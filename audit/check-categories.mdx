PX organizes audit checks into three classes:

- **Class 1** - cross-system validation. Compares the PMS's accounting engine against your business model. **Available on Guesty only** (OwnerRez has no built-in accounting engine).
- **Class 2** - intra-data consistency. Validates that PMS-reported numbers reconcile against themselves and against your tax/commission configuration.
- **Class 3** - business model validation. Validates that the business model's expectations hold across reservations.

PX runs checks in tiers within each class:

- **Tier 0 (canonical)** - taxes, fees, commissions. The core revenue checks.
- **Tier 1 (deterministic findings)** - guest-driven fees (pet, extra guest).
- **Tier 2 (signal-driven)** - keyword detection in notes (pet, event, party).
- **Tier 4 (OTA anomalies)** - channel-specific oddities (Airbnb total mismatches).

## Guesty checks

Guesty has a built-in accounting engine, so PX runs all three classes.

### Class 1 - cross-system validation

- **AD ledger balance** - Guesty's accommodation deposit ledger balances.
- **Ledger balance O+AP+C=0** - Guesty's owner + accounts payable + cash sum to zero.
- **Journal entry date validation** - journal entry dates fall within reservation dates.
- **Balance / cash / AD mismatches** - the various Guesty internal balances reconcile.
- **Missing AD entries** - bookings expected to have accommodation deposit entries but lack them.

### Class 2 - intra-data consistency

- **Tier 0 - taxes** - amount, applicability (inclusive/exclusive, per-stay/per-night), stay-length filters, channel sync.
- **Tier 0 - fees** - presence, amount calculation.
- **Tier 0 - commissions** - rates against channel commission configuration.
- **Tier 1 - missing pet fee** - `numberOfPets > 0` but no pet fee charged.
- **Tier 1 - missing extra-guest fee** - `guests > accommodates` but no extra-guest fee charged.
- **Tier 2 - notes scan** - keywords in notes/specialRequests trigger NOTES_SUGGEST_PET, PET_FEE_NEEDS_REVIEW.
- **Tier 4 - Airbnb total mismatch** - HIDDEN_OTA_FEE when Airbnb total does not equal expected total.

### Class 3 - business model validation

Only runs when the property has an assigned active business model.

- **Generated ledger balance** - the PX-generated ledger from your business model balances.
- **Owner statement reconciliation** - calculated owner payout matches recorded payout.
- **Recognition date validation** - charges land on the dates your business model specifies.
- **Commission rate vs config** - commission rate matches the business model's commission_setup.

## OwnerRez checks

OwnerRez has no accounting engine, so Class 1 does not apply. Class 2 and Class 3 do all the work, with additional data-integrity checks compensating.

### Class 2 - intra-data consistency

All Tier 0/1/2 checks from Guesty plus:

- **Revenue integrity** - sum of charges equals booking total amount.
- **Charge split** - on each charge, owner amount + commission amount = charge amount.
- **Zero rent** - active booking has accommodation charge > 0.
- **Missing payment** - balance > $0.01 but booking is not flagged unpaid.
- **Overpayment** - guest paid more than the booking total.
- **Negative balance** - balance is negative.
- **Cancellation audit** - canceled bookings have appropriate refunds (or are flagged as unrefunded).
- **Overlapping bookings** - two confirmed bookings on the same property overlap.
- **Phantom booking** - past departure, $0 paid, status still active.
- **Commission rate drift** - same property + channel pair has multiple commission rates across bookings.
- **Tax calculation mismatch** - tax math does not reconcile against catalog rules.
- **Payment / refund totals** - PAYMENTS_TOTAL_MISMATCH, REFUNDS_TOTAL_MISMATCH.
- **Orphan refund** - refund without a matching payment.
- **Duplicate payment** - two payments that look like the same transaction.
- **Deposits with non-zero adjustments** - bank deposit lines with unexpected adjustments.

### Class 3 - business model validation

Same set as Guesty, but built from per-charge data instead of journal entries:

- Generated ledger balance.
- Owner payout reconciliation.
- Recognition date validation.
- Commission rate vs business model.

### Catalog short-circuit

If the OwnerRez catalog has no confirmed/user-added rows for a relevant kind, the audit emits a single `CATALOG_EMPTY` finding instead of running per-reservation checks. Confirming at least one tax row clears the short-circuit.

## What does *not* get checked

PX does not check:

- Pricing decisions (was this rate optimal?).
- Channel performance (which channel is most profitable?).
- Forecasting or pacing.
- Marketing, reviews, or guest communication quality.

PX is an accounting and trust audit tool. Pricing and revenue management belong elsewhere.

## Adding new checks

PX's check framework is extensible. New finding types ship in product updates - watch for release notes. If you have a check you wish PX ran, write to support@pxaccounting.com with the use case and an example reservation that should fire it.
