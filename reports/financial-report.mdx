The **Reports** page generates reservation-level financial breakdowns - revenue, expenses, fees, taxes, payouts - across any check-in date range. Filter, sort, customize columns, and export to CSV.

</Note>

## Generate a report

Two date pickers and a button:

- **Check-in From** - earliest check-in date to include.
- **Check-in To** - latest check-in date to include.
- **Generate Report** - kicks off the report.

Reports are generated on demand against your PMS. Larger date ranges take longer to load. Initial open of the page auto-fetches the current month if the feature is enabled.

If you hit your PMS's rate limit, the page shows a 429 banner with a countdown. The Generate Report button is disabled until the cooldown ends.

## Filter and display

Once a report loads, more controls appear:

- **Property** - dropdown of all properties; pick one to filter.
- **Status** - dropdown of statuses present in the report.
- **Revenue Metric** - which revenue figure to use in the summary cards (Total Payout, Accommodation, Cleaning, Taxes, etc., plus dynamic fees).
- **Columns** - popover with grouped checkboxes (Reservation, Financial, Fees, Accounting, Details). Pick which columns to show. Includes **All** and **None** toggles. Persisted to local storage.
- **Export CSV** - download the visible rows.

## Summary cards

Four KPI cards at the top:

- **Revenue Metric** - the metric you chose (default: Total Payout). Sum across the report.
- **Total Bookings** - count of reservations.
- **Avg Nightly Rate** - average of accommodation / nights across the report.
- **Balance Due** - outstanding balance across the report. Has a tooltip explaining what is included.

## Customizing columns

Click the **Columns** button. The popover groups columns by category:

- **Reservation** - Check-In, Check-Out, Code, Listing, Guest, Status, Channel.
- **Financial** - Total Payout, Subtotal, Balance Due, Total Paid, Cash Balance.
- **Fees** - Accommodation, Net Accommodation, Cleaning, dynamic fee columns.
- **Accounting** - Total Taxes, Adv. Deposit.
- **Details** - Nights, Guests, Confirmed.

Toggle individual columns or use **All** / **None** at the top of each group. Your selection is saved to local storage and persists across sessions.

## Exporting CSV

Click **Export CSV**. PX downloads a CSV with the visible columns and rows. Filters and sort order are preserved in the export.

The CSV uses comma delimiters and UTF-8 encoding. It opens cleanly in Excel, Google Sheets, and Numbers.

## Empty state

If the date range has no reservations, the page shows an empty state: *"No Reservations Found"*. Widen the range or change the property filter.

## Status filter behavior

Inquiries are excluded from the main report by default - they show up in a separate count next to the total. To include them, look for the **Status** filter and select Inquiry along with whatever other statuses you want.

## Rate limit handling

If your PMS returns a 429 (rate limit) during report generation, PX:

1. Surfaces a banner with the cooldown countdown.
2. Disables the Generate Report button until the countdown finishes.
3. Cancels the in-flight request.

Wait for the countdown and click Generate again.

## When to use reports vs audits

- **Reports** - "show me the revenue and payouts across these reservations." Operational view.
- **Audits** - "tell me what is wrong with these reservations." Diagnostic view.

Run an audit before generating an owner statement to catch issues. Run a report to build the statement itself once the audit is clean.
